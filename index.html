Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers

What is included in the membership? +

Our membership includes access to all gym equipment, group fitness classes, locker facilities, and mobile app features. Premium plans include personal training sessions and nutrition consultations.

Can I freeze my membership? +

Yes! You can freeze your membership for up to 2 months per year for medical reasons or extended travel. Contact our support team to process your freeze request.

Do you offer free trial? +

Absolutely! We offer a 7-day free trial so you can experience our facilities, meet our trainers, and see if FitLife is the right fit for you.

What are your operating hours? +

We're open Monday to Friday from 5:00 AM to 11:00 PM, and weekends from 6:00 AM to 10:00 PM. Elite members have 24/7 access with their membership card.

Can I cancel my membership anytime? +

Yes, you can cancel your membership with 30 days notice. There are no hidden fees or long-term contracts. We believe in earning your membership every month.
